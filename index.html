Welcome to Fediverse Instance Alliance Network

We are a network of fediverse instances. FIAN is mean Fediverse Instance Alliance Network, because It's a Allliance of Fediverse Instances, like Mastodon, Misskey, and Pleroma.
If you want to join, join the Discord server or this forum topic. After that, just put the following text on the front page.

We are member of FIAN (Fediverse Instance Alliance Network).
